About us

LymeNB is the registered business name for the New Brunswick Lyme Disease Association Inc., an incorporated not-for-profit organization as of August 2016.

LymeNB's purpose is preventing tick-borne illnesses while supporting and being a voice for those who are affected.

In September 2016, a volunteer board of directors was appointed to set the direction for the corporation and to oversee its operations.
